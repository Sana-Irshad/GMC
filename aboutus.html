Our Chief Operating Officer Mr. Tariq Amin, has over 20 years of extensive experience in the auditing and accounting industry. His far-seeing vision, leadership, and motivation have enabled many organizations to achieve greater heights, where he has been partnered or associated with. His vast experience in performing audits and analyzing financial statements will help you to put aside all the worries related to accounting and financial management and focus on your business growth.
